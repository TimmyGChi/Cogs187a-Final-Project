Hunger on Wheels' desire is to bring the amazing street food culture of San Diego to you each and every weekday. We select the freshest and tastiest food trucks around to provide you with a fun, delicious, and new culinary experience for lunch. Each day we will host a rotation of popular tucks in a variety of flavors and cuisines, both sweet and savory to tantalize your taste buds and provide you with an entertaining and unique lunch experience. We hope you enjoy Hunger on Wheels!
