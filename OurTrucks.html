Casanova Fish Tacos

CASANOVA Fish Tacos objective is to bring to the customer the real Baja style fish taco experience. The blend of spices used in the batter has been a 3 year process to become perfected. The famous and most flavorful spicy pico de gallo which is customer favorite, the tomatillo based salsa with habanero for that extra kick and 3rd place winner at the 2010 Del Mair Fair and the secret white sauce are all original recipes created from scratch.
